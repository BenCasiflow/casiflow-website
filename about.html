About

Built by a player, for players.

Casiflow exists because the tools players actually need didn't exist. So we built them.

Our Story

It started with a simple question.

There's nothing quite like the feeling of a good session. The rush of a big win, the satisfaction of walking away up, the anticipation of the next time you play. If you're a casino player, you know exactly what that feels like.

But here's the thing nobody talks about — most players have no idea whether they're actually winning. Not really. They remember the good sessions and forget the bad ones. They know they deposited last week but can't remember how much. They have accounts at four different casinos and couldn't tell you their combined position if their life depended on it.

I was one of those players. I tried spreadsheets, notes on my phone, everything. Nothing gave me the honest, combined truth across every platform. So I built Casiflow — a tool that tells you exactly where you stand, across every casino you play at, all in one place. Because every serious player deserves to know their real numbers.

Our Mission

Level the playing field.

The house has always had the data advantage. Casinos know exactly how much you've deposited, how much you've withdrawn and what your lifetime net position is. They use that data to understand you better than you understand yourself.

Casiflow changes that. We give you the same clarity the house has always had — your real numbers, across every casino, in one place. Not to stop you playing. Not to lecture you. But because a player who knows their numbers is a smarter, sharper, more in control player. And that's exactly what you deserve to be.

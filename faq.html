General

Getting Started

Getting Started

Click Create My Free Account, enter your email address and choose a password. That's it — you're in.

Once you're logged in, go to the Casinos section and click Add Casino. Enter your casino name, add all your deposits and withdrawals to build your history, and enter your current balance. Press Add Casino and it will be added to your dashboard.

Features

Features

Your net result is your total withdrawals minus your total deposits, plus your current balance across all casinos combined. It tells you whether you are actually up or down overall — the real number most players never know. That's why it's important to keep your deposits, withdrawals and current balance up to date at all times.

You can set a monthly deposit limit and a monthly net loss limit in your profile. These limits track your activity across every casino you play at combined — not just one. Your dashboard will warn you when you are approaching your limits.

Goals let you set personal targets for your gambling — for example a monthly budget, a profit target or a session limit. Casiflow tracks your progress automatically and keeps you accountable.

At the end of each month Casiflow generates a summary of your activity — total deposited, total withdrawn, net result and which casinos you played at. It's a clear snapshot of how your month went.
